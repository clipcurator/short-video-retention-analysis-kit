# Hook And Pacing Review

Review the first seconds, information rhythm, caption density, and payoff timing of a short video.

## When To Use This

Use this guide when the team needs a clearer decision process before production, publishing, or iteration.

## Decision Areas

- Input quality and source context
- Audience promise and expected viewer reaction
- Production effort and review cost
- Platform or release constraints
- Risks that should be caught before work scales
- Feedback that should inform the next cycle

## Practical Checklist

- The goal is written in one sentence.
- The audience or viewer situation is clear.
- The output format is defined before production starts.
- Review criteria are visible to everyone involved.
- Known risks are listed before final approval.
- The final notes explain what should change next time.

## Common Mistakes

- Starting production before the decision criteria are clear.
- Treating AI output as final without human review.
- Mixing strategy, production, and QA in the same unclear document.
- Forgetting to save the reasoning behind a decision.
