# Contributing

This repository is maintained as a neutral practical resource. Contributions should make the workflow clearer, more reusable, or easier to review.

## Good Contributions

- Better checklists
- Clearer templates
- Realistic examples
- Additional review criteria
- Notes that help teams avoid repeated production mistakes

## Editorial Rules

- Keep the tone neutral and knowledge-first.
- Do not turn the repository into an advertisement.
- Avoid absolute claims such as "best", "guaranteed", or "number one".
- If a tool is mentioned, explain its fit and its boundary.
- Prefer short paragraphs and copy-ready templates.
